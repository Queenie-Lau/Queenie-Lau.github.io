About

Hi, I'm Queenie! I'm an incoming sophomore at UC Berkeley intending to major in Computer Science and minor in Sustainable Design. My interests are all over the place, but I enjoy editing videos, bullet journaling, design, and coding!

I am currently a Mobile App Developer Intern at Dandilyonn working on creating an iOS application dedicated to educating youth about the dire effects of climate change. In the summer of 2019, I was part of Google's Computer Science Summer Institute program (Google CSSI) and built Lucidity, an interactive web application that allows users to study efficiently with flashcards utilizing the spaced repetition method.
